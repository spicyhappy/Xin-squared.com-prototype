About

I’m Xin Xin, an interaction designer and developer. My work uses technology to help others experience different emotions and see from another point of view. I believe that beautiful and thoughtful design will improve the world.

I was born in China and immigrated to the U.S., where I studied film and animation at the Rhode Island School of Design and now live in Cambridge. Currently, I lead design at Panjiva, where I use my visual and research skills to make global trade easy to understand. Personally, I’ve been exploring gameplay and narrative in virtual reality.

I love super spicy food and traveling to new places.

Xin Xin makes things, asks questions, dreams big, eats very spicy Chinese food, and loves, loves, loves to learn.
